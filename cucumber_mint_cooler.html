Cucumber Mint Cooler Recipe

Refreshing Cucumber Mint Cooler

The Cucumber Mint Cooler is a crisp and rejuvenating beverage made with fresh cucumber, mint, and lime. Perfect for a hot day or as a refreshing drink at any time!

Ingredients (Serves 2)

  • 1/2 cucumber, sliced
  • 10-12 fresh mint leaves
  • 1 tbsp honey or simple syrup (optional)
  • 1 tbsp fresh lime juice
  • Club soda (or sparkling water)
  • Ice cubes
  • Mint sprigs and cucumber slices for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a glass, add the cucumber slices and mint leaves.
  2. Muddle the cucumber and mint together gently to release their flavors.
  3. Pour in the lime juice and honey or simple syrup, if desired.
  4. Fill the glass with ice cubes.
  5. Top with club soda (or sparkling water) and stir gently.
  6. Garnish with mint sprigs and cucumber slices.
  7. Serve and enjoy your refreshing cucumber mint cooler!
