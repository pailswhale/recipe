Virgin Mojito Recipe

Refreshing Virgin Mojito

A virgin mojito is a classic non-alcoholic beverage made with fresh mint, lime, and soda water. It's a perfect drink to keep you cool and refreshed!

Ingredients (Serves 2)

  • 10-12 fresh mint leaves
  • 2 tbsp fresh lime juice
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • Ice cubes
  • Club soda (or sparkling water)
  • Lime slices, for garnish
  • Mint sprigs, for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a glass, add the mint leaves and sugar.
  2. Using a muddler or the back of a spoon, gently crush the mint leaves to release the oils.
  3. Pour in the lime juice and stir to combine.
  4. Add ice cubes to fill the glass halfway.
  5. Top off the drink with club soda and stir again.
  6. Garnish with a lime slice and a mint sprig.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy your refreshing virgin mojito!
