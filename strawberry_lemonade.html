Strawberry Lemonade

Refresh yourself with this sweet and tangy strawberry lemonade. Perfect for a summer day!

Ingredients (Serves 4)

  • 1 cup fresh strawberries, hulled
  • 1 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice (about 4 lemons)
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 4 cups cold water
  • Ice cubes
  • Optional: Fresh mint leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a blender, combine the strawberries, lemon juice, and sugar. Blend until smooth.
  2. Pour the strawberry mixture into a large pitcher and add the cold water. Stir to combine.
  3. Fill glasses with ice cubes and pour the strawberry lemonade over the ice.
  4. Garnish with fresh mint leaves if desired and serve chilled.
