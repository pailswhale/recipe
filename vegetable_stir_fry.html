Vegetable Stir Fry Recipe

Healthy Vegetable Stir Fry

This colorful and healthy vegetable stir fry is a quick and delicious meal, packed with a variety of vegetables and an irresistible soy-based sauce.

Ingredients (Serves 4)

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 bell peppers, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 cup snow peas
  • 2 medium carrots, julienned
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon grated fresh ginger (optional)
  • Cooked rice, for serving

Instructions

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the onion and cook for 2-3 minutes until softened.
  3. Stir in the bell peppers, broccoli, snow peas, carrots, and zucchini. Cook for 5-7 minutes until the vegetables are tender but still crisp.
  4. Add the garlic and cook for 30 seconds until fragrant.
  5. In a small bowl, mix together the soy sauce, sesame oil, rice vinegar, honey (or maple syrup), and grated ginger (if using).
  6. Pour the sauce over the vegetables and toss to coat evenly. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes until everything is well coated and heated through.
  7. Serve the stir fry over cooked rice and enjoy!
