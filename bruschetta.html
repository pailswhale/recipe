Bruschetta Recipe

Classic Bruschetta

This classic Italian appetizer features fresh tomatoes, basil, and garlic, served on crunchy toasted sourdough bread for a delicious bite.

Ingredients (Makes 4 servings)

  • 1 loaf sourdough bread
  • 3 medium tomatoes, diced
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh mozzarella (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Slice the sourdough bread into 1-inch thick slices and place them on a baking sheet. Toast in the oven for 5-7 minutes until golden brown and crispy.
  3. In a medium bowl, combine the diced tomatoes, basil, and garlic. Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic vinegar, then season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Top each slice of toasted bread with the tomato mixture.
  5. If using, add a slice of fresh mozzarella on top of each bruschetta, and place the tray under the broiler for 1-2 minutes until the cheese melts.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy!
