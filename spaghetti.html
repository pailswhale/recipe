Spaghetti Recipe

Classic Spaghetti

This classic spaghetti recipe features a rich tomato sauce with garlic, basil, and parmesan, perfect for a satisfying meal. Simple yet delicious!

Ingredients (Serves 4)

  • 1 lb spaghetti pasta
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil, chopped (optional)
  • Parmesan cheese, grated (for topping)

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Add the crushed tomatoes to the pan along with the dried basil, red pepper flakes, salt, and pepper. Stir well and simmer for 10-15 minutes, allowing the sauce to thicken.
  4. Once the sauce is ready, add the cooked spaghetti to the pan and toss to coat the pasta with the sauce.
  5. Serve the spaghetti with fresh basil and grated parmesan cheese on top.
  6. Enjoy your classic spaghetti!
